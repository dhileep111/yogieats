5 Simple Steps to Reduce Screen Time Before Bed

Blue light destroys your melatonin production. If you scroll before sleep, you are telling your brain it is noon, not midnight. Here is the YogiEats protocol to reclaim your sleep:

  • 1. The "Phone Foyer" Rule: Charge your phone in the kitchen or living room. Never in the bedroom. Buy a standard alarm clock.
  • 2. Grayscale Mode: Turn your phone screen to Black & White in accessibility settings. It makes Instagram instantly boring.
  • 3. The 30-Minute Gap: No screens 30 minutes before bed. Use this time for hygiene or stretching.
  • 4. Eye Yoga: Palming (rubbing hands and placing over eyes) relieves digital eye strain instantly.
  • 5. Replace, Don't Remove: You can't just "stop" scrolling. Replace it with a book or a breathing exercise.
