About Me

Mindful Living in a Digital World

Namaste. I am a dedicated Yoga practitioner passionate about helping you find peace in the chaos of modern life.

My journey into Yoga wasn't just about physical flexibility; it was about finding mental clarity. In a world constantly demanding our attention through screens and notifications, I found that the ancient tools of Yoga, Pranayama (breathwork), and mindful eating are the ultimate "Digital Detox."

I created YogiEats to share practical, simple ways to integrate these ancient practices into your busy schedule. Whether you need to fix your sleep, manage stress, or simply learn to breathe again, I am here to walk that path with you.
